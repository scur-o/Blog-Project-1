About Me

a glimpse of my life.

During COVID lockdown, I had the opportunity to rethink my life. I questioned what I've done throughout my life and what I truly wanted to do. As expected, my goals did not align with what I have been doing. I gave more thought to the end-goal of my life and realized how limited time I have in this world to accomplish what I want. I was lost, but empowered by this urgent desire to change, I picked up my first book in years. To my surprise, I fell in love with reading and learning. This blog is therefore, a collection of my thoughts, interests, and learnings through various genres of books including, but not limited to philosophy, psychology, economics, computer science, technology, and science.

My primary intention for this blog is to store my thoughts; to review my growth through time. Though in the future, I do wish to share my work to anyone else who may be traveling in a similar path as I.
